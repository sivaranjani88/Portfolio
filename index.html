Project-1

Project: Mr & Mrs Cottage Industry(Real-time Website)

Mr & Mrs Cottage Industry is a leading herbal haircare and skincare brand with over 35,000+ happy regular customers across India. I developed a fully responsive, real-time e-commerce website that showcases the brand's natural product range while providing a seamless online shopping experience. The website enables customers to explore products, learn about their herbal benefits, and place orders securely through an intuitive interface. This project helped strengthen the brand's digital presence and improve customer engagement by combining modern web technologies with a user-friendly design.

Project-2

Project: Sri Vedha Multi-Speciality Hospital

Sri Vedha Multi-speciality Hospital is a modern healthcare institution committed to delivering quality medical care through a wide range of specialized services. I developed a fully responsive, real-time hospital website featuring online appointment booking, doctor profiles, department information, health packages, emergency contacts, and patient enquiry forms. The website provides patients with quick access to essential healthcare information while ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience across all devices. This project enhanced the hospital's digital presence by improving accessibility, patient engagement, and the overall efficiency of online healthcare services.

Project: GetFit Gym

GetFit Gym is a modern fitness center focused on promoting a healthy and active lifestyle through professional training programs and state-of-the-art facilities. I developed a responsive single-page landing website using Bootstrap, designed to showcase the gym's services, membership plans, trainers, and contact information. The landing page features a clean, visually appealing layout with smooth navigation and mobile-friendly responsiveness. This project demonstrates my ability to create attractive and performance-optimized landing pages that effectively engage visitors and support business promotion.
